Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Meet & Greet, and is it required?

Yes, a Meet & Greet is required for all new clients before we finalize any booking. It is a free, 30-minute consultation in your home where we can meet you and your pets, run through their routines, review paperwork, and verify house instructions. It ensures your pet is comfortable with us and that we are a perfect match.

Are you insured and bonded?

Absolutely. We are fully insured and bonded specifically for pet sitting and in-home animal care. Your peace of mind and your pets' safety are our highest priorities.

What happens if my pet has a medical emergency while you are sitting?

In the event of an emergency, we will immediately transport your pet to your preferred primary veterinarian (or the closest 24-hour emergency animal hospital if necessary). We will contact you immediately. The pre-filled Veterinary Treatment Release form authorizes us to seek medical care and lists your pre-approved veterinary spending authorization limits.

What species of pets do you care for?

We care for all kinds of pets! While dogs and cats make up a large portion of our clients, we are highly experienced with daily habitat care for birds (parrots, finches), reptiles (tortoises, geckos, bearded dragons), fish, and small companion mammals (rabbits, guinea pigs, hamsters). Each pet gets a tailored routine based on their species-specific needs.

Do you offer overnight stays in my home?

We do offer overnight pet sitting services! Overnights provide standard evening and morning routines, feeding, walks, plenty of cuddle time, and the added benefit of home security while you are away. Availability is limited, so we recommend booking well in advance.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that travel plans can change unexpectedly. For standard pet sitting visits, we request at least 48 hours' notice for cancellations to receive a full refund. Cancellations made with less than 48 hours' notice may be subject to a fee. Overnight stays require a 7-day notice for a full refund of deposit.
